Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo pasta is a delicious and creamy pasta dish that is sure to please everyone at the dinner table. This classic Italian-American recipe consists of tender chicken breast cooked to perfection, served atop a bed of al dente fettuccine pasta, and smothered in a rich and velvety Alfredo sauce.

The chicken is seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices and cooked until golden brown and juicy. The Alfredo sauce is made with butter, cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and a pinch of nutmeg, resulting in a smooth and decadent sauce that perfectly coats the pasta and chicken.

The combination of flavors and textures in this dish is simply mouth-watering. Each bite of tender chicken, creamy pasta, and rich Alfredo sauce is a perfect balance of savory and indulgent flavors that will leave you feeling satisfied and content.

Chicken Alfredo pasta is perfect for a comforting family dinner or a cozy date night in. It's easy to make, yet elegant and delicious, making it a go-to recipe for any occasion. So grab your apron, head to the kitchen, and get ready to impress your loved ones with this irresistible Italian classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 pound of fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook 1 pound of fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, season 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast with salt and pepper, and cook in a large skillet over medium heat until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter over medium heat. Add 4 cloves of minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Slowly pour in 2 cups of heavy cream, whisking constantly to combine with the butter and garlic.
  5. Bring the cream to a simmer and let cook for 5-7 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.
  6. Stir in 1 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to coat in the sauce. Let cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the chicken through.
  8. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the sauce and chicken. Toss to combine.
  9. Serve the Chicken Alfredo pasta hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
