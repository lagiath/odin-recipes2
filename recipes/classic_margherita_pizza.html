Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish that is loved for its simplicity and fresh, flavorful ingredients. This pizza is made with a thin crust that is crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, and is topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves.

The tomato sauce is made from ripe, juicy tomatoes that are cooked down to a thick, rich sauce with a touch of garlic and olive oil. The sauce is then spread over the pizza dough, and topped with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese. The cheese melts beautifully in the oven, creating a gooey, creamy layer on top of the pizza.

To finish off the pizza, fresh basil leaves are scattered over the top, adding a fragrant, herbaceous note to the dish. The result is a delicious, satisfying pizza that is both light and flavorful.

Margherita pizza is a perfect meal for any occasion, whether it's a quick weeknight dinner or a fun weekend gathering with friends and family. It's a classic dish that never goes out of style, and is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-ounce) ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface until it's about 1/4-inch thick.
  3. Place the pizza dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Arrange the slices of fresh mozzarella on top of the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the pizza to taste.
  7. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Drizzle the pizza with extra-virgin olive oil.
  10. Slice and serve hot.
